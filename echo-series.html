Echo
A five-book fiction series by Scot Anderson
In the town of Raven’s Hallow, no one loses their freedom. They simply stop needing to use it.

Echo follows the quiet rise of systems designed to reduce friction, ease decision-making, and keep communities running smoothly.

It is not a story about collapse. It is a story about adaptation.
