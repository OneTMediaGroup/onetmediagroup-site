Land Acknowledgement

Respecting the land on which we live and create.

Today, we acknowledge the land on which we live and work. One T Media Group operates in the Town of Newmarket, situated on the treaty lands of the Williams Treaties First Nations. This area is the traditional territory of the Wendat (Huron-Wendat), Haudenosaunee, and Anishinaabe peoples. For thousands of years, these nations have cared for this land, traveled its waterways, and built community here. This region was part of the historic Toronto Carrying Place Trail — an essential Indigenous trade and travel route connecting Lake Ontario to Lake Huron. We recognize the enduring presence, resilience, and contributions of Indigenous peoples — past, present, and future. As creators and storytellers, we commit to learning, listening, and moving forward with respect.
