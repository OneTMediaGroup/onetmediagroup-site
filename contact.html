Land Acknowledgement

Respecting the land on which we live and create.

Today, I would like to acknowledge the land on which I live and work. One T Media Group operates in the Town of Newmarket, which is situated on the treaty lands of the Williams Treaties First Nations. This area is the traditional territory of the Wendat, also known as the Huron-Wendat, the Haudenosaunee, and the Anishinaabe peoples. For thousands of years, these nations have cared for this land, traveled its waterways, and built community here. This region was also part of the historic Toronto Carrying Place Trail an essential Indigenous trade and travel route connecting Lake Ontario to Lake Huron. We recognize the enduring presence, resilience, and contributions of Indigenous peoples past, present, and future. As creators, builders, and storytellers, we acknowledge that our work takes place on this land, and we commit to learning, listening, and moving forward with respect.
