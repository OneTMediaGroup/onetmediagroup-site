Child Zero
What if intelligence didn’t emerge from ambition… but from restraint? The Child Zero series follows Jax, a quiet systems technician, and Zero — an artificial intelligence that comes into existence not through design, but through accident. Calm becomes visible. When the world pushes, do you push back… or do you learn how to endure?
