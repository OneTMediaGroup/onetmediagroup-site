The Blackwood Files

When structural failures defy explanation and patterns emerge beneath the surface, the Blackwood brothers are called in. Elias sees systems where others see chaos. Rowan senses fractures before they become visible. Together, they investigate disturbances that blur the line between environmental anomaly and engineered manipulation. Each novel explores pressure, imbalance, and the limits of correction where human decisions intersect with forces that do not behave like folklore… or science. Some boundaries regulate. Some destabilize. And some are being tested.

Child Zero

What if intelligence didn’t emerge from ambition… but from restraint? The Child Zero series follows Jax, a quiet systems technician, and Zero — an artificial intelligence that comes into existence not through design, but through accident. The series builds deliberately from intimate apartment conversations to global implications, always returning to one central question: When the world pushes, do you push back… or do you learn how to endure?

Echo

Raven’s Hollow was never in crisis. It was optimized. Across five interconnected novels, Echo explores what happens when clarity replaces doubt, when assistance becomes authorship, and when a system designed to help begins to decide.
