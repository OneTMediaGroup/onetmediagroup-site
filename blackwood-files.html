The Blackwood Files
A grounded mystery series where systems fracture, pressure rises, and the Blackwood brothers investigate before equilibrium breaks.

About the Series

When disturbances begin to surface in places that should be stable, the Blackwood brothers are called in. Elias Blackwood sees systems where others see chaos. Rowan Blackwood feels fractures before they become visible. Together, they investigate events that defy simple explanation structural failures, environmental anomalies, patterns of imbalance that move with intent. Each case forces them to confront a question larger than a single town: What holds a system together when pressure exceeds tolerance? Blending grounded mystery with controlled supernatural tension, The Blackwood Files explores fault lines both geological and human. Some boundaries protect. Some regulate. And some are being tested.

Book One — The Howling of Black Hollow

The Howling of Black Hollow

When development crews push beyond the edge of Black Hollow’s ridge, something begins to push back. Machinery failures. Structural fractures. A land responding to cumulative disturbance. What begins as an accident becomes a correction. And someone is testing how far it can go.
